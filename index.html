Smart Watch for Calendar Emulator final version

Welcome to our smartwatch calendar!

To start the calendar, click the grey square labeled "Calendar App".
To make an appointment on a day, click on that day and fill in the details on the next screen.
To change month, "swipe" sideways on the screen by clicking, dragging, and releasing the mouse.
To view and delete appointments, click on "view app" in the top right hand corner.

We hope you enjoy using our app!
